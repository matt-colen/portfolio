About Me

Hi I'm Matt Colen, a passionate front-end web developer with a background in Operational Excellence (OpEx) and project management. I have a Bachelor's in Psychology and a Master's in Business Administration which has helped me build up solid analytical thinking, creativity, and business skills.

My Journey

My journey into web development began during my time in operational excellence management, where I discovered my passion for creating intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces. Since then, I've immersed myself in the world of front-end development, honing my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to bring ideas to life on the web.
